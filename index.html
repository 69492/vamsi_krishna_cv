⭐ Featured Project
01

SmartSeat ↗

Traditional booking treats a berth as all-or-nothing. SmartSeat introduces segment-wise berth allocation — a berth occupied Howrah→Gaya can still be booked Gaya→New Delhi, eliminating wasted capacity across every route.

  • Segment-wise VACANT/OCCUPIED model per route — not per train
  • CSP-based allocation engine dynamically fills seats in real time
  • Lightweight ML model ranks & recommends top-3 optimal seats
  • QR-code digital tickets with downloadable booking confirmation
  • Session-based Previous Bookings — zero authentication required
PythonHTML/CSS/JSCSP AlgorithmML Ranking
